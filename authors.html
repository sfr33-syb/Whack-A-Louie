Cheat Sheet / Tips & Tricks

  • Click quickly on moles to maximize points before they disappear.
  • First Boss appears at 200 points — focus on hitting it fast.
  • Second Boss appears at 400 points — it teleports randomly, be ready!
  • Final Boss appears at 700 points — minions will appear that reduce your score if clicked.
  • High score rankings: Beginner < Hunter < Boss Slayer < Elite Destroyer < Mole God.
