Whack-A-Louie: Boss Battle Edition

Game Description

Whack-A-Mole: Boss Battle Edition is an action arcade game where players click on moles as fast as possible to earn points. As your score increases, powerful bosses appear with unique abilities that make the game more challenging.

How To Play

  • Click on moles to earn 10 points.
  • Reach 200 points to unlock the First Boss.
  • Reach 400 points to unlock the Second Boss.
  • Reach 700 points to unlock the Final Boss.
  • Defeat bosses by clicking them until their health bar reaches zero.
  • Be careful — some bosses have special abilities!
  • Your final score determines your rank.
